Our Purpose

To work as a community to create a thriving business environment that will increase the quality of life for the residences of Tooele County. We want to build a community where members love to live and raise their families. We work towards promoting local businesses, amenities and attractions to help boost Tooele's economic outlook and prosperity. We provide announcements of upcoming events and local weather information to help residences and business owners be more aware and involved.
