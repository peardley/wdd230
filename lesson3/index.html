Site Purpose

The purpose of the Weather and More website is to provide weather related information, local events listings, and stories to the regions and communities of Southeast Idaho. It is a site where both locals and non-locals can go to get fun and informative information about this region. A home page will contain current weather forecasts and stories about the region along with navigation links to three communities within the region. Communities included on this site are Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, Idaho. Each of these communities will have a devoted page with current local weather conditions and forecasts, weather related news stories specific to that town, and event listings for that area. A photo gallery page will also be included as a way to showcase the people, landscapes, and events of this area. While weather is a key factor of this site, it contains so much more for the viewer to enjoy.
