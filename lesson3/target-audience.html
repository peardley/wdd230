Target Audience

Age Range and Gender

Southeast Idaho provides a variety of outdoor activities available for nearly all age groups. And while this site can benefit a large age span, the target age range this site will focus on is ages 20-50, both male and female. This age range tends to be more active outdoors and would rely on a website to help them understand what activities are available to them based on weather conditions. Because the website talks about outdoor and local community events, it can serve both men and women of varying educational backgrounds.

Persona 1

Name: Ben Fields

Occupation: Accountant

Demographics: Not Married, BS in Accounting, 27 years old

Hobbies: Loves fishing, hunting, sports cars, and being outdoors

Ben is from Salt Lake City, UT and will head over to the Fish Haven area seasonally to do some fishing. He enjoys bringing some of his buddies with him and making it a weekend trip. He would use this site to help him see the conditions of the river or reservior he wants to fish on as well as check the weather forecasts to decide which weekend to fish. Because the site offers tips and stories about the region, he is able to see which type of fish are biting currently so he knows what time of bait to purchase. He uses the photo gallery to show his friends the area to entice them to join him.

Persona 2

Name: Jenny Walters

Occupation: Homemaker

Demographics: Married, BS in Education, 35 years old, three kids.

Hobbies: Scrap booking, restoring furniture, gardening

Jenny is a mother that is always trying to keep her kids busy and outdoors. Jenny is local to the SE region of Idaho and uses the site frequently to see what events are happening around her. She loves to take her kids to local fairs and events to help them appreciate what is around them. She often likes to take them on short hikes to keep up them active. She uses the maps on the sites to find local hiking spots and uses the weather forecast to know how to prepare her kids for the hike.

Senarios

  1. My band and I would like to play at a local event. What kinds of events are in Preston and would the weather be good enough to play outdoors?
  2. I have job offer in Preston, Idaho? What do their seasons look like and what kind of activities could I expect to do there?
  3. Is there snow coming? Do I need to pull out my snowblower?
  4. How cold is it going to get at night for our camping trip to SE Idaho. Do I need to pack extra blankets?
  5. I'd like to plant a garden. When's the best time to start based on the weather in my area?
