***Public Announcement***

No Parking on Roadways November 1st to March 31st

Please remember the following to help the city with snow removal and be considerate of your neighbors.

Parking of motor vehicles, trailers, snowmobile, or other objects on any city street is NOT permitted from November 1st to March 31st regardless of the snow fall. Violations of the winter parking rules are an infraction with a fine of $100 to $300 for each occurance. Parking on Main Street in the business district is allowed during business hours.
